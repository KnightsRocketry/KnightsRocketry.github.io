Who We Are?

Knights Rocketry is a rising American Rocketry Challenge (ARC) team from Independence High School in Frisco. While we are a new rocketry team, our members have extensive experience in other prestigious STEM competitions, including bringing a team to the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) World Championship.

What is ARC?

The American Rocketry Challenge is the world's largest student rocket contest. [4] Teams design, build, and launch rockets to meet specific altitude and flight duration goals, providing hands-on experience in aerospace engineering. [3, 5] The top 100 teams compete at the National Finals for prizes and recognition. [4]

How Can You Help?

Sponsoring our team supports local STEM education and provides your brand with visibility. Your logo on our rocket and team shirts showcases your commitment to inspiring the next generation of innovators and leaders in science and technology.

The Challenge

'26 Competition Basics

This year's goal involves designing a rocket to carry a payload of raw eggs to a specific altitude and land within a precise time window without damaging the payload. [2, 6] This complex task requires careful planning, aerodynamic simulation, and rigorous testing.
