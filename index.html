About The Team

Who We Are?

Knights Rocketry is a rising American Rocketry Challenge (ARC) team from Frisco, Texas. While new to rocketry, our team builds on a strong STEM foundation through years of involvement in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), where our robotics team has qualified for the FRC World Championship in past seasons.

What is ARC?

The American Rocketry Challenge is the world's largest student rocket contest. Teams design, build, and launch rockets to meet specific altitude and flight duration goals, providing hands-on experience in aerospace engineering.

How Can You Help?

Sponsoring our team supports STEM education and gives your brand national visibility. Your logo on our rocket and shirts shows your commitment to innovation and inspiring the next generation of leaders.

The Challenge

This year's goal involves designing a rocket to carry two raw eggs to a specific altitude, returning it to Earth safely within a precise time window. This complex task requires careful design, simulation, and rigorous real-world testing to succeed.
