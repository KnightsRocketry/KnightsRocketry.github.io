First successful test launch!

Posted on October 26, 2025

What a day! We conducted our first full-scale test launch of the 'Odyssey I' prototype. The rocket achieved stable flight, and the parachute deployed successfully. We gathered crucial data that will help us fine-tune the design for the official competition. More details and photos to come!

The primary goal of this test was to validate our airframe's stability and test the dual-deployment recovery system. After reaching an apogee of approximately 850 feet, the drogue parachute deployed perfectly, followed by the main parachute at 400 feet for a soft landing. We couldn't be happier with the results.

A huge thank you to everyone who has supported us so far. This is a massive milestone, and we're more motivated than ever to prepare for the American Rocketry Challenge. Stay tuned for our next update where we'll analyze the flight data!
