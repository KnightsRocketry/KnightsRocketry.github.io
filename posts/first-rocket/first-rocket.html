Our First Rocket: Nebula One

Posted on July 25, 2025 Written by Anikait Rana

We're thrilled to announce that after a concentrated build session that started and concluded on July 23rd, our first rocket is officially complete! Meet the Nebula One.

Using the "Big Bertha" model, our team has created our first rocket to be launched tomorrow (July 26, 2025)!

The maiden flight of the Nebula One is at the Dallas Area Rocketry Society (DARS) field in Gunter, Texas. This launch will be a test of our craftmanship and work.

Thanks for following our build journey. We can't wait to share the launch results with you!
